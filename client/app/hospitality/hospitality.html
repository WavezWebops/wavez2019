Guidelines for Accommodation

  1. Participants need to apply before 14th March 5pm. Any requests after that would not be entertained.
  2. Accommodation will be provided from 16th March 2018 (11:00 AM) to 18th March 2018 (11:00 PM) only. These timings will be followed strictly.
  3. The payment can be done in prior online through UPI or bank transfer(IMPS).
  4. Early Bird Offer (Online Payment):
    1. Accommodation charge will be Rs.250 per person per day.
    2. An extra Rs.100 per person will be collected before check-in as caution deposit which will be refunded after the fest.
  5. On-spot Accommodation:
    1. Accommodation charge will be Rs.300 per person per day.
    2. An extra Rs.100 per person will be collected before check-in as caution deposit which will be refunded after the fest.
    3. Limited accommodation will be provided on the basis of first come, first serve.
  6. Accommodation will be charged as per the 12am-12am basis and not 24hr basis.
  7. The accommodation will be given to boys and girls separately at boys hostel and girls hostel.
  8. In case of any cancellation of accommodation booked online already, only half of the money will be reimbursed.
  9. A confirmation mail will be sent to the participant once the accommodation is approved. The participant has to carry a copy of the confirmation mail.
  10. Alcohols, Drugs and explosives of any kind are strictly prohibited inside the campus. Any item deemed unsafe will be prohibited.
  11. Accommodation will be strictly provided only to the students and not to anybody accompanying the students.
  12. Accommodation will be given on a sharing basis in common rooms which can accommodate 20-25 people. Single occupancy rooms will not be provided at any cost.
  13. We will be providing the students with a set of mattress, pillow and blanket which should be safely returned before checking out.
  14. Accommodation fee is for providing rooms only. Food is not included.
  15. Kindly keep an eye on your belongings.The participants are suggested not to bring any valuables. Wavez team or IIT Madras will not be responsible in case of any mishaps.
  16. Participants should vacate the allotted room and handover the items safely on the night of 18th March, 2018 itself. We will not provide accommodation till 19th March, 2018 morning.
  17. Even if you are attending in groups, each one of you should register individually and we will try our level best to accommodate all of you in the same common room if possible.
